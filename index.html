    About Me

    Background:

    I was born and raised in Newcastle, England. I've since lived in Wales, Thailand and Japan. I studied Psychology at Bangor University in Wales and Japanese Language at Waseda University in Japan.



    My Approach:

    With my background in educational psychology and my previous career in teaching, it has been important for me to develop skills that not only help people understand expectations, but also to have a comfortable, enjoyable and productive experience.


    This is why I would like to continue to grow and educate myself. I want to provide the smoothest and most comfortable experiences for anybody using what I develop from frontend to backend.


    My Projects

    Why a Quiz App?

    During my time as a teacher, I identified that a lot of my students struggled with time during tests.

    It seems they needed to practice answering quickly in a timed environment.

    Key Features

    1. Score tracking and updating as you go
    2. Final score display on end page
    3. Question number tracking and updating
    4. Timer with numbers and visuals
    5. Mandatory answers with timed out feature
    6. Multiple levels of difficulty
